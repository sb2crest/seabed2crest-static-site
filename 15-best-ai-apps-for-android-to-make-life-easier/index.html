Top 15 Best Free Artificial Intelligence Apps for Android Devices

Google Assistant

If you use Android phones, you‚Äôve probably heard of Google Assistant at least once. Google Assistant is a voice-controlled virtual personal assistant developed by Google. As one of the Google AI-based Android applications, most smart devices currently come pre-installed with Google Assistant, so you don‚Äôt need to download it from the Google Play Store.

By using natural language technology and machine learning, this AI app for Android understands users‚Äô voice commands and then provides relevant responses. Google Assistant even helps users make life easier by integrating other tools used for daily tasks like making appointments, playing music, setting reminders, and looking up information right on search engines, etc.

Bing AI

Microsoft Bing has long been known as a direct competitor to Google in the search engine market.¬†According to Statista, if Google is currently the dominant search engine with a market share of up to 84%, Bing, in recent years, has put up an admirable fight with a nearly 9% market share in the PC space.

Bing‚Äôs expansion continues beyond this point. Recently, Microsoft Bing has incorporated ChatGPT – an AI chatbot developed by OpenAI into its search engine to provide users with more conversational and personalized search results. Bing, from a normal search engine, turned into a smart search engine called Bing AI/Ms. Bing with the help of artificial intelligence.

Similar to ChatGPT, Bing AI is a tool that integrates AI with the Bing browser to answer questions from users. A special feature of Bing AI is that it supports continuous updating of the latest data, while ChatGPT is limited to data until 2021. Bing AI tool helps users answer questions and create content quickly and is extremely convenient.

Replika AI

Replika AI is an AI chatbot and virtual app that uses AI tech and machine learning to provide you with a personalized and conversational experience. Unlike conventional AI chatbots that only give accurate answers based on user questions, the app is designed to serve as a companion for users, allowing them to talk about their thoughts and feelings in a safe and non-judgmental space.

By using natural language processing and¬†deep learning algorithms, Replika AI is capable of understanding and responding to user queries in a human-like manner. The longer you talk, the better and more realistic the conversation between you and Replika AI will be. This AI application also includes other features, such as mood tracking and meditation exercises, helping users to improve their mental health and well-being.

Lensa AI

If you are a social media lover, you better not ignore Lensa AI – an all-in-one photo editing app that is powered by artificial intelligence. Launched in 2018 by Prisma Labs, Lensa AI is one of the popular AI applications for image and video post-editing available on both Android and IOS devices.

With Lensa AI capabilities, you can get flawless edits based on your real-life selfies in just one tap. This AI app offers users a wide range of features, including filters, effects, auto-enhancement, object recognition, and background removal. However, Lensa AI only really left a strong impression when it launched a feature called Magic Avatars in 2022. By allowing users to turn any image into unique images that almost look cartoon-like, Magic Avatars plays a role as a smart AI image generator and creates a new trend on social networks for a while.

Google‚Äôs Socratic

Socratic is a Google product developed to help users learn and solve curriculum and homework-related questions. Using Socratic is very simple. Students post any questions on this AI app. Socratic will use AI tech to recognize the problem and then provide a detailed explanation of the answer.

In addition, Socratic also contains a vast knowledge base of most subjects today, such as math, literature, physics, science, etc., supporting users to improve their overall knowledge and skills. These resources can be in the form of video tutorials, quizzes, or even practice problems.

Socratic has both a free version and a paid subscription. However, they have no functional difference. The only difference is that the paid subscription gives users a more seamless learning experience without having to be distracted by ads or other promotional content.

ELSA Speak

ELSA (English Language Speech Assistant) Speak is the right choice for individuals who want to improve their English-speaking skills. ELSA Speak uses advanced speech recognition and deep artificial intelligence to analyze the user‚Äôs voice and provide real-time feedback on their rhythm, pronunciation, and intonation.

Users only need to choose one of the lesson categories available on the app, and they will immediately be provided with a series of exercises and drills by the app to practice and hone their speaking skills. Whether you are a student, a professional, or someone who needs to improve their English language skills, ELSA Speak can help you achieve your goals early.

Freeletics

Freeletics is an AI fitness application for Android that helps busy users of all fitness levels to effectively access training programs and workout plans. Acting as a personal trainer, Freeletics provides you with 20 unique Training Journey of exercises and workouts, including cardio, strength training, and stretching. Every stage of the user‚Äôs training journey is analyzed by the AI-powered personal trainer to provide the optimal solution for each different fitness focus.

In addition to workout plans, Freeletics also acts as a nutritionist that provides users with meal schedules and recipes to support their fitness goals. The recipes and nutrition menu of this AI app includes a wide variety of dietary options, from vegetarian and vegan, to paleo diets.

Wysa

Wysa is a mental health care app using modern AI technology designed and developed by Touchkin eServices. Similar to a chatbot, Wysa uses a number of therapeutic techniques, such as cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), to provide users with 150 different therapeutic techniques through chat conversations. Thereby managing, minimizing, and even eliminating user anxiety, stress, and depression.

For the free version, users can use Wysa‚Äôs standard tools, such as guided meditations, breathing exercises, and mindfulness practices. To have access to the entire library of tools and receive extra support from one-on-one sessions with a coach, you must subscribe to a premium version.

Duolingo

Want to learn a new language but don‚Äôt know where to start? Duolingo – a language learning app – is here to give users a fun and enjoyable language learning experience. Despite being a completely free educational platform, Duolingo offers users online courses in up to 40 different languages.

Instead of providing traditional courses, this app uses game-like elements, such as points, levels, badges, etc., to simplify language learning. After each successful level, Duolingo rewards users with scores for completing lessons and achieving milestones.

Duolingo courses are referenced and correspond to the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) – an international standard of language proficiency. In addition to language teaching, Duolingo also offers short assessments after learners complete a Unit in the course. Through this assessment, not only can learners review and test their knowledge, but Duolingo also has a basis for evaluating the effectiveness of its teaching and making necessary improvements.

Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft is expanding its ecosystem with new AI innovations. Following the success of Bing Chat – a conversational AI that is part of Microsoft‚Äôs Bing search engine, the company continues to mark its position by launching a new product called Microsoft Copilot.

Rather than a separate AI solution, Microsoft Copilot is an innovative AI feature integrated into Microsoft 365 applications like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. Users can simply ask questions, and Copilot will quickly find the most relevant answers to fulfill their commands. The tool is even capable of analyzing the context of the data being worked on, thereby providing the most relevant suggestions, summarizing information, and¬†creating data visualizations¬†based on distinct insights of each customer.

Using Copilot appropriately, in combination with the inherent usefulness of Microsoft Suite, you make complex tasks more accessible, reduce the cognitive load associated with digital work, thus increasing productivity and efficiency as a whole.

Amazon Alexa

Are you looking for a personal virtual assistant that is as powerful as Siri but operates on a platform other than iOS? Amazon Alexa is the answer.

Developed by Amazon, Amazon Alexa is an AI assistant that responds to voice commands. By giving Alexa voice commands, users can automate simple tasks from turning off lights and turning on security systems to playing music, setting alarms, and providing weather updates.

The tool is integrated and available on most Amazon Echo devices and even extends to third-party smart speakers, appliances, and other gadgets. Thanks to its broad smart home compatibility, Amazon Alexa is the top choice for home automation, helping people easily manage their living spaces and improve their quality of life to a new level.

Gencraft

AI algorithms are evolving at a dizzying pace in the current technology landscape. Once a tool for providing quick information, AI models now have the capability to transform words and images into unique art.

Gencraft is an AI art generator that is especially suitable for designers looking for creative inspiration and non-professional users who need to use simple images directly for personal purposes. With Gencraft, there is no barrier to user creativity. This smart application, in addition to providing numerous templates and tools to simplify the design process, also has the ability to create elaborate structures, compositions, and figures from text prompts.

Gencraft is compatible with both Android and iOS operating systems, making it easy to download to any mobile device and use it anywhere with just an Internet connection.

Cleo

Having a headache over monthly expenses? Cleo is here to help.

As an AI-powered financial assistant for all users, Cleo is here to make managing your finances more efficient than ever. It can link directly to users‚Äô bank accounts and act as an experienced money coach. Using AI and natural language generation, Cleo offers a wide range of features to help you manage a personal budget through valuable personalized insights into spending and bills, improving your financial habits in the long run.

AI Mirror

AI Mirror is a photo editing application with integrated AI technology designed for image transformation purposes. From an existing photo in your mobile device, for example, AI Mirror allows users to turn it into an artistic masterpiece with just a few simple steps. A diverse collection of image styles, filters, and animation themes is one of the most important supporting features that help you complete your own unique artwork.

Rather than creating an entirely new image from scratch, AI Mirror enhances users‚Äô images with AI-driven artistry and is especially suitable for tech-savvy individuals who need to diversify content on popular social media platforms like Instagram, Discord, etc.

Google Lens

The list of free AI apps for Android ended with Google Lens. Developed by Google, Google Lens is a visual search engine that uses machine learning and computer vision technologies to analyze and understand images and objects in the real world. Based on the characteristics of the elements on the image you provide, Google Lens gives the closest search results about the origin, definition, and information related to the subject of the original image.

What is more, Google Lens also has the ability to coordinate with other Google applications, such as Google Assistant and Google Photos, to handle work. For example, by pointing the camera towards the row of SSID labels on the back of the router, users can easily connect to Wi-Fi automatically. This process is divided into two stages. In it, Google Lens will be responsible for information processing, and Google Assistant will be responsible for activating Wi-Fi.
